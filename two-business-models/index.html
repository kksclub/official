Two business models to jump in China market

Fresh Brands

If your brand has lower awareness in China and intent to touch base with China market

In the KFC Model the merchant can put the product in KKS overseas warehouses. This is relatively an easier model to test new products in the market. The merchant will take responsibility to deliver the products into the KFC warehouse where from all aspects of delivery will be handled by KKS. Hence businesses which have low brand awareness but intent to test China market could use this model.


KKS Overseas Fulfillment
Benefits for suppliers
  • 100% access to China SMEs who seeking overseas products
  • Low cost market entry for testing and assortment optimization
  • No extra sales/operational costs
  • Flexible settlement options: weekly or monthly in home currency
  • Easy return/exchange of inventory in the home country

Hot Brands

If your brand has high awareness in China, and you are planning increasing investment in China market

Here the brands can ship their fast moving products into China. This is the most cost effective way of selling imported goods. The customer experience is also better due to faster last mile delivery. However, it does not make sense to put high number of non-moving SKUs in bonded warehouse especially when there is a a problem of products reaching expiry. Hence this model is mostly suitable for top sellers.


KKS Direct Import
Benefits for suppliers
  • 100% access to Chinese consumers and SMEs seeking overseas products
  • Supply chain depth /risk control
  • Direct collaboration with a powerful distribution channel
  • Save delivery time
